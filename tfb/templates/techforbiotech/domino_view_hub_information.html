How does it work

Emblem is required

Within registration process you need to choose the emblem you would like to be associated with, although a subemblem is optional and you can pick it up later in your profile panel. If you are a professional who wishes to connect with others who represent your category, it is advisible to pick up the subemblem, ex: Green Biotech. On the other hand, if you do not want to express your belongness to a specific category, you can skip a subemblem.
